Get Involved with SpringFest 2025

SpringFest 2025 is just around the corner, and we’re looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help make this year’s festival a success! Whether you’re passionate about event planning, have a creative flair, or just want to be part of a fun and lively community celebration, there’s a role for you. By volunteering, you’ll help bring people together and ensure that SpringFest is an unforgettable experience.

Volunteer Roles for SpringFest 2025

We have a wide variety of volunteer roles available, so no matter your skills or interests, there’s something for everyone. Here are some of the opportunities you can be part of:

  1. Event Setup & Tear Down Crew

    2. Join the behind-the-scenes action by helping us set up before the event and clean up afterward. You’ll be essential in getting the venue ready and ensuring everything is packed up after the fun is over.

  2. Guest Services & Information

    3. Welcome attendees, provide directions, and ensure everyone has a fantastic time at SpringFest. If you love interacting with people, this role is perfect for you!

  3. Vendor Assistance

    4. Support our festival vendors by helping them with setup, booth management, and providing excellent service to customers. You’ll help create a seamless experience for both vendors and festival-goers.

  4. Entertainment & Activity Assistants

    5. Help run the many fun activities and entertainment at SpringFest! Whether you’re guiding guests at interactive booths or helping manage live performances, you’ll keep the energy high and the fun flowing.

  5. Social Media & Marketing Support

    6. Spread the excitement of SpringFest before, during, and after the event. Help document the festival through photos, videos, and social media posts, engaging our audience online.

  6. Parking & Traffic Control

    7. Ensure that parking runs smoothly and attendees can safely enter and exit the event. If you’re good with logistics and enjoy working outdoors, this role is for you.
